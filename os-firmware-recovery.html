Operating System Deployment & Firmware Recovery

Technical Project | Device Recovery & Troubleshooting

Business Value

Restored full device functionality and ensured compatibility across refurbished systems to support seamless user operations and reduce downtime.

Project Responsibilities

  • Executed clean operating system reinstalls and configured custom local user accounts on refurbished tablet devices.
  • Diagnosed and resolved system driver conflicts, manually deploying and updating firmware to ensure hardware compatibility with peripheral devices such as Bluetooth keyboards.
  • Troubleshot seized hardware and internal cooling systems, managing motherboard and heatsink assemblies to resolve thermal throttling and hardware faults.

Outcome

Delivered stable OS performance, improved device reliability, and ensured refurbished units met operational standards for daily use, enabling redeployment at zero hardware replacement cost.

Skills Used

  • OS Installation & Configuration
  • Firmware Deployment
  • Driver Troubleshooting
  • Thermal Diagnostics
  • Hardware Repair
