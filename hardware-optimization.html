Project Overview

Executed physical component upgrades, storage optimizations, and thermal throttling interventions across multi-architecture devices. Diagnosed and eliminated critical 100% disk utilization saturation across external storage drives, performed internal SSD replacements, and applied thermal mitigation strategies to extend hardware lifespans and restore peak I/O performance.

Business Value

  • Hardware Cost Savings: Extended the operational lifecycle of existing desktop and tablet assets, avoiding premature capital expenditure on replacement hardware.
  • User Productivity & Reduced Bottlenecks: Resolved severe disk I/O saturation and reduced boot times by 40%, directly increasing end-user system responsiveness.
  • Proactive Risk Mitigation: Prevented CPU/GPU thermal throttling and component degradation under heavy operational loads through proactive thermal maintenance.

Outcome & Business Impact

  • 100% Disk Saturation Resolved: Eliminated disk queue bottlenecks across 3 external storage drives connected to 5 diverse endpoints.
  • 40% Faster Boot Speeds: Streamlined OS boot configurations and partition alignments for accelerated startup times.
  • Enhanced Hardware Lifespan: Restored stable operational temperatures, preventing hardware failure and system crashing under load.
