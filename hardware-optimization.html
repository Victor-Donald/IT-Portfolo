PC Hardware Optimization & Storage Upgrade
Improving system responsiveness and reliability through targeted hardware and storage enhancements.
SSD Replacement Disk Partitioning Storage Optimization Reliability Gains

Overview

This project focused on extending the lifespan and improving the performance of refurbished and personal-use computer systems. By upgrading storage hardware and tuning disk configurations, I aimed to deliver faster boot times, smoother application performance, and fewer hardware-related failures.

Technical Objectives

  • Replace aging mechanical drives with SSDs to improve performance.
  • Configure disk partitioning and alignment for optimal throughput.
  • Reduce device failures and instability across multiple endpoints.
  • Validate system health through hardware diagnostics and testing.

Execution

I performed internal hardware maintenance, including cleaning and inspecting components, followed by SSD installation and migration of existing data where appropriate. Disk partitioning was configured to support both OS and data volumes, with alignment checks to ensure optimal performance.

After the upgrades, I ran hardware diagnostics and monitored system behavior to confirm improved responsiveness and stability. This process was repeated across multiple devices, creating a consistent optimization pattern for refurbished systems.

Outcome

  • Noticeably improved boot times and application responsiveness.
  • Reduced device failures and instability across upgraded endpoints.
  • Extended hardware lifespan without incurring full replacement costs.
  • Established a repeatable hardware optimization workflow.
