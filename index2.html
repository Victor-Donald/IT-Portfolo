Enterprise Hardware Deployment & OS Specialization | June 2025 – Present

  • Technical Execution: Architected a standardized deployment framework to transition a diverse hardware fleet from generic Windows 11 environments to optimized local configurations. Successfully deployed tailored Surface Recovery Images for specialized endpoints, including Surface Pro 7+ and Surface Go 2 tablets, as well as enterprise-grade HP and Lenovo desktop systems.
  • Driver Injection & OS Modification: Built and deployed custom bootable media using advanced command-line and imaging tools. Dynamically injected device-specific hardware drivers into master deployment files, ensuring 100% out-of-the-box compatibility for peripheral chipsets, network interface cards (NICs), and integrated touch sensors.
  • Storage Provisioning & Media Management: Engineered high-capacity storage deployment media, implementing file-system formatting protocols across 2TB Western Digital My Passport and 1TB Buffalo external drives. Configured fully optimized, secure bootable drives to support offline local user setups and rapid system recovery.
  • Business Value & Outcome: Eliminated dependency on active internet connections during deployment, reduced terminal provisioning times, and established a scalable, cross-platform disaster recovery baseline across multiple hardware architectures.
